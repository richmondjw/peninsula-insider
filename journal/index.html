What we’re writing about this season

Autumn on the Peninsula is vintage season on the ridge and fireplace-lunch season everywhere else. The Journal is leaning into the service pieces that help you build a weekend around those two moods - plus a cellar-door dispatch series, honest stay notes, and the rolling Peninsula This Weekend brief refreshed each week.

Every piece below answers a real question a weekend visitor actually asks. If we can’t finish the sentence “Read this before you…” then we don’t publish it.
