From the editor’s desk

What we’re writing about this season

Autumn on the Peninsula is vintage season on the ridge and fireplace-lunch season everywhere else. The Journal is leaning into the service pieces that help you build a weekend around those two moods - plus a new cellar-door dispatch series, honest stay notes, and the weekly Peninsula This Weekend picks every Wednesday at 6 pm.

Every piece below answers a real question a weekend visitor actually asks. If we can’t finish the sentence “Read this before you…” then we don’t publish it.

Formats in this issue

  • Service pieces - the practical guides: weekend shapes, rainy-day plans, family trips
  • Long lunch lists - the daylight-dining playbook
  • Cellar door dispatches - the wines, the producers, the arguments in the vines
  • Weekend Picker - one pick, one backup, one skip - every Wednesday 6 pm
  • Stay notes - the rooms that change the shape of a weekend
  • Insider edits - shortlists with a point of view
