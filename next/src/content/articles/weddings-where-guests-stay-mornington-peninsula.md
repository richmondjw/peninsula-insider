## The most common planning mistake

Couples spend months on the venue and two weeks on guest accommodation. By then the best options are gone, guests are scattered across inconvenient locations, and the morning-after brunch requires three different Ubers.

This guide works backward from your venue area to tell you where guests should actually stay, what the realistic options are at different budget points, and what to sort out first.
