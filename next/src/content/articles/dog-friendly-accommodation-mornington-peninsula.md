## How to think about booking

    
The strongest Peninsula dog stays are usually houses, cottages, and holiday accommodation rather than traditional hotel inventory. That is partly a policy issue and partly a space issue. Owners should expect to verify fencing, indoor rules, bedding/furniture rules, and any pet fees before committing.


    
## Best use cases

    
**For beach-first owners:** stay in a zone that keeps Tassells Cove, Somers, or Mornington-side options within easy reach.

    
**For wine-and-lunch owners:** the right Red Hill / Main Ridge base can work, but only if the property itself is not high-friction with the dog.

    
**For the easiest overall weekend:** prioritise access and dog practicality over the most prestigious address.
