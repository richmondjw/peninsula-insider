## The short version

Bushrangers Bay is the walk we point people to when they want to see the wilder side of the Peninsula without committing to a full-day hike. The track drops from the Cape Schanck lighthouse precinct through coastal scrub to a broad crescent of basalt and sand that feels much further from Melbourne than it is.

**Distance:** 5km return  
**Duration:** 1.5–2 hours (return)  
**Grade:** Moderate  
**Dogs:** Not permitted (Mornington Peninsula National Park)  
**Parking:** Cape Schanck Lighthouse carpark, Cape Schanck Rd  
**Best seasons:** Autumn, winter, spring  
**Best time of day:** Morning or late afternoon (avoid midday in summer)
