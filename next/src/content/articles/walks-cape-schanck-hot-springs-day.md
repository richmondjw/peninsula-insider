## The day, in sequence

The Mornington Peninsula Hot Springs are 25 minutes north of Cape Schanck. Most people drive past the lighthouse on the way somewhere else, or treat the springs as a standalone destination without a reason to be at the southern end of the Peninsula first.

This page fixes that.

**Morning:** Bushrangers Bay Walk (2 hours return) or Cape Schanck Boardwalk (45 minutes)  
**Midday:** Lunch near Cape Schanck or drive north toward Fingal  
**Afternoon:** Peninsula Hot Springs, Fingal (pre-booking essential)  
**Drive back to Melbourne:** From Fingal, approximately 80 minutes in non-peak conditions
