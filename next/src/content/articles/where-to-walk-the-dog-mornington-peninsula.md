## The standout

    
**Briars Community Forest** is one of the most useful owner assets on the Peninsula because it is not pretending to be something it is not. It is practical, dog-first, and large enough to justify the drive if you simply need a good run rather than a scenic social outing.


    
## How to choose the right walk

    
**Choose the beach walk** when the weather is good and the dog needs space.

    
**Choose the fenced or reserve-style walk** when control matters more than scenery.

    
**Choose the short foreshore or town-edge walk** when the dog is part of a larger Peninsula day rather than the whole plan.
