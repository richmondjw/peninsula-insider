Sorrento Gelato

The Ocean Beach Road gelato window - classic Italian flavours, a rotating seasonal list, and a compulsory stop on any summer evening stroll

Sorrento Gelato · Sorrento

Sorrento Gelato is the window on Ocean Beach Road that everybody in Sorrento joins the queue for at least once per visit. The flavours are classic Italian done properly - pistachio, stracciatella, hazelnut, seasonal fruit rotations - and the queue moves fast. There is nothing more to say. It is excellent gelato in exactly the right location on the Peninsula's most cheerful main street.

Go after dinner. Get two scoops. Walk toward the front beach.
