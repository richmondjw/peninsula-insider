Thai Orchid

The Peninsula's default Thai - a long-running Main Street room with properly aromatic curries and honest weeknight pricing

+61 3 5975 8299
Thai Orchid · Mornington

Thai Orchid is the Peninsula's default Thai and has been for years. The dining room is unpretentious, the menu is the proper full Thai list - green and red curries, a massaman that actually tastes of cardamom and cinnamon, pad thai that is not drowned in sugar, a whole fish with chilli and tamarind when the kitchen feels like it - and the weeknight prices make it the easy answer when you want dinner in Mornington and do not feel like another bottle of Peninsula Pinot.

Book on Fridays and Saturdays; walk in Tuesday through Thursday. The staff know the regulars by name and the room fills with the kind of low-tempo neighbourhood dinner crowd that tells you exactly what kind of place this is.

Order the massaman. Order a stir-fry. Share everything.
