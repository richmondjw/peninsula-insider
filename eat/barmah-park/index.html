Barmah Park Farm Kitchen

All-day farm kitchen and courtyard - brunches, cheese plates, and an easy Peninsula midweek lunch without the wine country theatre

Reserve a table +61 3 5978 8049
Barmah Park Farm Kitchen · Moorooduc

Barmah Park runs a beautifully relaxed farm kitchen and courtyard alongside its more formal canape-paired cellar-door program. The kitchen opens for brunch and runs an all-day menu that is closer to a village café than a winery restaurant - house-baked pastries, good eggs, cheese and charcuterie boards, a short pasta list at lunch, and a glass of estate Pinot on the side if the mood is right.

The courtyard is the reason to come. Vines visible through the planting, slow country birds on the fence, and the kind of pace that makes a Monday feel like a holiday. For anyone who wants a winery day without the formal long lunch commitment, this is one of the Peninsula's better options.

Go on a weekday if you can. Take the kids, take the dog (check first), stay for a cheese plate after the eggs.
