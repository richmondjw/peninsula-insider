Via Boffe

A small warm Italian room tucked into the hinterland - handmade pasta, a short hand-written list, and the Peninsula's best neighbourhood trattoria

Reserve a table +61 3 5989 6111
Via Boffe · Main Ridge

Via Boffe is the Peninsula trattoria that deserves much more attention than it gets. The room is small and warm - polished concrete, low lighting, an open kitchen that you can see from every seat - and the menu is a short hand-written list that changes with whatever the team feels like cooking that week. Handmade pasta is the headline, but the antipasti is where the kitchen really wakes up: fresh ricotta with olive oil and cracked pepper, grilled peaches with stracciatella, a wood-fired flatbread that disappears in three minutes.

This is the Peninsula version of the Italian room everyone wishes they had in their own suburb. The wine list leans into small Italian producers alongside a short Peninsula local shelf, and the team is genuinely happy to steer you toward something unexpected.

Book ahead - the room is tiny and it fills fast on weekends. Sit at the counter if you are a pair, order three pastas between you, and finish with the hazelnut torta.
