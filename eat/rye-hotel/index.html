Rye Hotel

An enormous foreshore pub a short walk from Rye's front beach - family-friendly, deck-oriented, and the right answer on a warm afternoon

Reserve a table +61 3 5985 2277
Rye Hotel · Rye

The Rye Hotel is enormous in the best possible sense - a sprawling foreshore pub that absorbs families, locals, and long weekend crowds without anybody noticing the place has filled up. The main deck catches the afternoon sun, the kids' menu is honest, and the kitchen does the straightforward things well enough that nobody complains. The beer garden fills on a hot Sunday; the bar absorbs the overflow.

This is not a fine-dining destination and nobody involved is pretending otherwise. The point is the position - a short walk from the front beach, a deck with enough shade, and a menu that lets the kids eat something they recognise while you work through a schooner and look at the bay.

Good for the broad, low-key kind of Peninsula afternoon that has nothing to prove.
