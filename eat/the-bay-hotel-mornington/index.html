The Bay Hotel Mornington

A vibrant gastropub in a heritage-listed former bank - a main bar, an upstairs bandroom, and private dinners in the building's old vault

Reserve a table +61 3 5975 7199
The Bay Hotel Mornington · Mornington

The Bay Hotel occupies a heritage-listed former bank building on Mornington's main street, and the architecture alone is worth the visit. The ground-floor bar sits under the original tin ceiling and arched windows, the upstairs bandroom runs live music most weekends, and the building's old vault has been converted into the Peninsula's most characterful private dining space - bookable for small groups who want somewhere genuinely different.

The food is gastropub done with an actual point of view: locally sourced, handled with more care than the usual country-pub list, and paired with a strong bar program that leans into craft beer and Peninsula wine in equal measure. Live music turns a Friday night into something closer to a small inner-city venue than a Peninsula town pub.

Book the vault for birthdays. Come for the bands on Friday. The most characterful venue in the Mornington town centre.
