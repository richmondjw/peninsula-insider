Point Leo Wine Terrace

The casual, all-day option at Point Leo Estate - wood-fired flatbreads, cheese boards, and Point Leo wines by the glass on one of the Peninsula's most spectacular outdoor decks

Reserve a table +61 3 5989 9011
Point Leo Wine Terrace · Merricks

Point Leo Wine Terrace is the casual, drop-in face of the sprawling Point Leo Estate - no booking-six-weeks-out commitment, no degustation, no fuss. Walk in, take a table on the deck, order a cheese board and a glass of the estate Chardonnay, and let an hour or two go by while the sculpture park sits in the foreground and the bay stretches out beyond it.

The food is deliberately simple: charcuterie, cheese, wood-fired flatbreads, a share-plate list built around what the kitchen garden is producing. The wine program is the estate's own. The deck, with its open view back across the sculpture park toward the ocean, is one of the Peninsula's genuinely great outdoor rooms.

Use the terrace as your mid-morning reset on a Point Leo day, or as your complete lunch when you want the estate experience without committing to a full restaurant booking.
