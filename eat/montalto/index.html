Montalto Vineyard & Olive Grove

Single Vineyard Pinot Noir and a Sunday lunch beneath the olive grove canopy

Montalto Vineyard & Olive Grove · Red Hill

Montalto is the Peninsula at its most painterly. Drive in and the estate opens up - lake, sculptures, kitchen garden, olive grove - before you've reached the cellar door. This is the place you bring visitors to prove that Victoria does wine country properly.

The restaurant earns its hat honestly. Pasta made daily, produce grown on-site or sourced from neighbours within five kilometres, and a wine list anchored by the estate but ranging thoughtfully. The terrace in summer is close to perfect.

Arrive early for a cellar door tasting before lunch. Set aside the afternoon.
