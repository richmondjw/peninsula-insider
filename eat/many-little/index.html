Many Little

Sri Lankan flavours in a European wine-bar setting - from the Polperro team, rotating alongside natural wines and small producers

Reserve a table +61 3 5989 8888
Many Little · Red Hill

Many Little is the Peninsula's most unexpected fine-dining cross-pollination. The project comes from the Polperro winery team, the setting is a European-style wine bar on the ridge, and the menu is built on Sri Lankan flavours and techniques - curry leaves, tamarind chutney, pickled lime, coconut, hoppers, spiced sambols - plated with the discipline of a modern dining room rather than a suburban curry house.

The combination should not work and it absolutely does. The wine list leans natural and small-producer, deliberately chosen to stand up to the bright, tangy, spice-forward flavour profile, and the pairing conversations with the team are one of the genuine pleasures of the room. There is nothing else like it on the Peninsula right now.

Book for dinner. Order the full sharing menu if it is available. Try the curry leaf cocktail.
