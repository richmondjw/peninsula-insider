Eldridge Estate

David Lloyd's tiny cult estate - single-clone Pinot Noirs from individual rows, plus Gamay, for the Burgundy obsessives

Book a tasting +61 3 5989 2644
Eldridge Estate · Red Hill

Eldridge is where Peninsula Pinot nerds go to geek out. David Lloyd runs the estate almost single-handedly, makes the wine himself, and produces single-clone bottlings - MV6, PTG, 115, 777 - in quantities so small that most of them are sold out by word of mouth before they reach a shelf. The cellar door is deliberately low-key: a shed, a small counter, Dave behind it, the wines lined up for you to taste one against the other.

The MV6 is the benchmark bottling, the one you'll still find on the best Melbourne wine lists and the one that quietly reminds everyone what a Peninsula Pinot can be. The Gamay is arguably Australia's most serious take on the variety. Tastings by appointment - call ahead - and keep an eye out for the tiny Clonal Comparison flight if it is available.

This is not a place to bring a wedding party. It is a place to arrive alone or as a couple, pay attention, and leave with a bottle you'll open when you want to remember the afternoon.
