Stillwater at Crittenden

Lakeside dining under a pergola at one of the Peninsula's founding wineries

Reserve a table +61 3 5981 9555
Stillwater at Crittenden · Dromana

Crittenden was there before most of the cellar doors that now define the Peninsula, and Stillwater is its quietly excellent dining room - set beside a still lake under a pergola strung with vines. The room's best trick is that it feels entirely unhurried.

The menu is produce-forward without the preachy apparatus, and the wine list is a generous walk through the estate's own catalogue with smart additions from elsewhere. Order the burrata, then whatever fish the kitchen is running that week. Take the lake walk afterwards.

A reliable weekday lunch - one of the best mid-priced meals on the plateau.
