Best Seafood on the Mornington Peninsula

The Peninsula's seafood scene is concentrated around its waterfront towns and fishing piers. Pier Street Kitchen in Flinders is the strongest all-round seafood dining room — one GFG hat, a proper kitchen, and a setting that justifies the southern drive. The Baths at Sorrento is the waterfront experience, and Flinders Pier Takeaway is the benchmark for a genuinely good casual stop that does not pretend to be anything else.

The Peninsula's fish is primarily sourced locally or from the southern Victorian coast — king george whiting, calamari, abalone in season, and Mornington Peninsula oysters when available. The category rewards finding the right venue for the right kind of afternoon.

Frequently asked questions

Where is the best seafood on the Mornington Peninsula?

Pier Street Kitchen (Flinders) holds one GFG hat and is the strongest all-round seafood dining room. The Baths at Sorrento is the best waterfront seafood experience. Flinders Pier Takeaway is the local institution for fish and chips.

Where can I get fish and chips?

Flinders Pier Takeaway — the pier location, fresh fish, no-frills format. Afloat in Mornington is the better sit-down version.

Are there dog-friendly seafood venues?

The Rocks at Mornington (outdoor deck) and Portsea Hotel (beer garden) are confirmed dog-friendly and serve seafood menus. Verify before visiting.
