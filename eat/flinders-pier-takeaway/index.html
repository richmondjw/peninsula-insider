Flinders Pier Takeaway

A parcel of flake and chips from the village, eaten on the historic wooden pier with weedy sea dragons drifting under the boards

Flinders Pier Takeaway · Flinders

Flinders Pier Takeaway is less a venue and more a ritual. The flake-and-chips parcel from the village takeaway, walked down to the historic wooden pier, eaten sitting on the boards while the weedy sea dragons drift underneath in the clear water - this is one of the Peninsula's best small food moments and it costs less than twenty dollars.

The pier is a working heritage structure and the dragons are one of the Peninsula's genuine natural wonders. The takeaway is honest, unpretentious, and exactly right for the context. Do not try to turn it into a meal. Treat it as what it is: a perfect bad-weather plan B and a sunny-day plan A.

Check tides; the underwater viewing from the pier is better on a falling tide with clear water.
