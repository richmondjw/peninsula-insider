Ten Minutes by Tractor

Estate-grown pinot noir alongside a seasonal degustation rooted in the Ridge vineyard

Reserve a table +61 3 5989 6455
Ten Minutes by Tractor · Red Hill

Three hats and the most considered wine program on the Peninsula - Ten Minutes by Tractor earns every accolade with a degustation that feels genuinely rooted in place. The Ridge vineyard slopes outside the window aren't decoration; they're the story. Book the long lunch: you'll want the afternoon.

The cooking is technically precise without being cold. The team understands restraint - flavours are clear and confident, not overwrought. The charred bread with cultured butter has become a quiet institution.

Book at least six weeks ahead for weekends. Lunch is the move.
