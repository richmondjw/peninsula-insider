Max's at Red Hill Estate

A long-standing Peninsula favourite perched above the Red Hill Estate vineyards - sweeping hinterland views and modern Australian cooking leaning hard on Peninsula produce

Reserve a table +61 3 5931 0177
Max's at Red Hill Estate · Red Hill

Max's has been doing the Red Hill Estate long lunch for decades, and the view from the dining room - the slope dropping away toward Western Port with French Island in the middle distance - is still the reason most people book. The kitchen runs a reliable modern Australian menu that leans into Peninsula produce and pairs directly with the estate's Chardonnay and Pinot Noir.

This is not the most ambitious kitchen on the ridge, and it is not trying to be. It is a well-run winery restaurant with one of the Peninsula's great views and a clear understanding of what people book it for: a relaxed lunch on a sunny day, matched to the bottle you just bought at the cellar door, with enough time in the afternoon for a second glass.

Book for a sunny weekend lunch. Arrive early. Work through the sparkling flight before the mains arrive.
