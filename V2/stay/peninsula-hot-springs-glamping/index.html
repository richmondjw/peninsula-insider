Peninsula Hot Springs Glamping

Eco lodges and glamping pods inside the Peninsula Hot Springs precinct - wake up, walk thirty seconds to the Spa Dreaming Centre

Peninsula Hot Springs Glamping · Cape Schanck

The glamping-and-lodges precinct at Peninsula Hot Springs is the closest thing the Peninsula has to a genuine wellness retreat stay. The eco lodges and canvas pods sit within the hot springs grounds itself - not a hotel nearby, not a separate resort, but physically inside the precinct - which means that the thirty-second walk to the Spa Dreaming Centre first thing in the morning, before any day guests have arrived, becomes the most memorable part of the stay.

The accommodation is pitched at the eco-glamping end of the market: comfortable beds, private decks, minimal noise, a deliberate absence of the resort-scale amenities that would distract from the point of being here. The point is the bathing - early, late, and often - and the food, spa treatments, and quiet that go with it.

Book a two-night package if you can. Arrive mid-afternoon, bathe at dusk, eat dinner at the on-site Amphitheatre restaurant, and go back to the pools before bed. One of the Peninsula's essential overnight experiences.
