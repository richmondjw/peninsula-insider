The Balnarring Pub

A proper country pub in the middle of Balnarring village - open fires in winter, a shaded beer garden in autumn, and honest counter meals

The Balnarring Pub · Balnarring

The Balnarring Pub is the kind of country local the Peninsula still does well - a proper pub in the middle of a proper village, with open fires in winter and a shaded beer garden in autumn. The kitchen runs honest counter meals built on Peninsula produce, the tap line runs through Peninsula breweries, and the clientele is a mix of locals, market-going weekenders, and the occasional Melbourne couple who have stopped on the way down to Flinders.

It is not trying to be a gastropub and it is not trying to be a winery restaurant. It is trying to be a good pub, and it succeeds. Come for the roast on a winter Sunday. Come for the beer garden after the Saturday market. Come for a weeknight parma when you are based at Balnarring and do not want to drive anywhere.

Locals' favourite that visitors keep rediscovering.
