Stringer's of Mornington

A Main Street institution - part grocer, part bakery, part wine shop, part tea rooms - and the first stop on any serious Mornington provisions run

Stringer's of Mornington · Mornington

Stringer's is the institution at the heart of Mornington's Main Street and the first place you go when you need to stock a weekend on the Peninsula in one stop. It is genuinely unclassifiable: part grocer, part artisan bakery, part wine shop, part tea room, all of it under one roof and all of it done well enough that locals have been using the place as their weekend base for decades.

The pastry case and bread wall are the headline. The cheese and charcuterie counter is the second reason to come. The wine shelves lean into Peninsula producers alongside a serious European selection, and the tea rooms at the back are a legitimate stop for a pot of something and a slice of cake on a rainy afternoon.

You will leave with more than you meant to buy. That is the correct outcome.
