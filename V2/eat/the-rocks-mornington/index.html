The Rocks Mornington

Perched at the end of Mornington Pier with near-360° bay views - a seafood-heavy menu and one of the most romantic tables in town at sunset

The Rocks Mornington · Mornington

The Rocks sits at the end of Mornington Pier and has one of the best locations on the whole bayside - the dining room reaches close to 360 degrees of bay view and the terrace at sunset is among the most romantic tables in town. The kitchen leans seafood: oysters, whole fish, pan-seared scallops, a steady list of things that came out of the water that morning or the morning before.

The cooking is more classical than adventurous, and that is the right call for the room. The bay is the spectacle; the plate is the support act. The wine list is sensible and leans into Peninsula producers where possible.

Book the window table for an early-evening reservation in autumn. Arrive in time for the sun to drop over Mount Eliza. Order the oysters, the whole fish, and something cold from the list.
