The Baths Sorrento

Sorrento's most iconic waterfront dining room — perched on the sand with Port Phillip Bay at your feet, and a seafood-first menu

The Baths Sorrento · Sorrento

The Baths sits directly on the sand at Sorrento — the kind of position that, anywhere else in the world, would be roped off for a hotel group. Here it is an open-air seafood dining room with Port Phillip Bay at your feet, and on a clear summer evening it is one of the most photographed meals on the Peninsula for a reason.

The menu leans into local fish and oysters done with restraint, a serious raw bar for starters, and whole fish or bouillabaisse for the middle of the table. A casual kiosk on the jetty side handles walk-up food and drinks if you do not want to commit to a full sit-down meal; the restaurant proper handles the long lunches and sunset bookings.

Book for sunset. Order the oysters and whatever whole fish the kitchen is running. Let the room do the rest.
