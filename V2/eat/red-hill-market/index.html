Red Hill Market

Victoria's finest artisan craft market, held the first Saturday of each month — more than 200 stalls of craft, produce, plants and peninsular makers

Red Hill Market · Red Hill

Red Hill Market is the single monthly event that pulls the whole Peninsula together. Held on the first Saturday of each month from September through May, the market spreads across the Red Hill Recreation Reserve with more than 200 stalls of handmade craft, gourmet produce, plants, artisan food, and the kind of small-run peninsular makers who do not have retail shops anywhere else.

The market is big, unapologetically so, and the best way to experience it is to commit to the full morning. Arrive by 9am for parking, work through the food stalls first, circle back for the plants and the craft, and leave before the late-morning crush kicks in. Coffee from one of the mobile roasters; a breakfast sandwich from the Balnarring Bakehouse stall; honey from a ridge apiarist; a bunch of ranunculus from the Merricks flower grower.

One of the Peninsula's essential weekend experiences. Plan a whole weekend around it if you can — the cellar doors are quieter after midday, and the market sets the mood for the rest of the day.
