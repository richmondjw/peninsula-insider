Sourdough Kitchen

A small-batch sourdough baker at the top of Mornington's Main Street — long-fermented loaves, a tight pastry line, and the best bread in town

Sourdough Kitchen · Mornington

Sourdough Kitchen is the Mornington baker that wine-room people quietly recommend when someone asks where the best bread in town is. The loaves are long-fermented, the crust is proper, and the tight line of pastries in the cabinet — croissants, morning buns, a Danish or two — is pitched at people who actually care about the detail. The coffee is good. The room is small. The locals are loyal.

This is a bread-first shop, and that is its charm. Buy a loaf, a couple of pastries, and a bottle of something from Stringer's up the road, and you have the bones of a Peninsula picnic.

Go early on weekends — the morning bun routinely sells out before 11.
