Cedar & Pine

Modern Middle Eastern grazing in a warm Mornington room - dips, charred flatbreads, slow-cooked lamb, and a table built for sharing

Cedar & Pine · Mornington

Cedar & Pine is the Mornington answer to the modern Middle Eastern dining-room movement that has swept the Melbourne scene over the past few years. The kitchen runs a grazing-style menu - hummus and labneh with house-made flatbreads, smoked eggplant, spiced carrots, slow-cooked lamb shoulder built for the centre of the table - and the room is warm and relaxed rather than fussy.

This is the Peninsula's best group dinner for a party where nobody can agree on what they want to eat. The menu is designed for sharing, the kitchen is generous, and the flavour profile will appeal to anyone from the kids on the end to the parents working through a second glass of the house red. The wine list is well-built and leans into Peninsula producers who suit the food.

Book for six or more. Order half the shared menu. Arrive hungry.
