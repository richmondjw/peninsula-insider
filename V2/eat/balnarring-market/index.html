Balnarring Market

A relaxed village-scale monthly market on Balnarring's green — local produce, makers, and the Peninsula's most family-friendly market morning

Balnarring Market · Balnarring

Balnarring Market is the smaller, softer cousin of Red Hill Market — a village-scale monthly event on the Balnarring Village Green that runs on the third Saturday of each month through the warmer months. The stalls lean more toward local produce and smaller craft makers and the whole event moves at a slower pace than the Red Hill crowd.

This is the market to take visiting family to when you want the produce-and-craft experience without the Red Hill parking situation. It is also the right warm-up for anyone who has booked a lunch at the Balnarring Pub or a cellar-door session at Quealy afterwards.

Go late morning, buy what looks good, and drift into a long Balnarring afternoon.
