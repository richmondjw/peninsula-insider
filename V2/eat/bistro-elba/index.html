Bistro Elba

A beachy, artfully dilapidated French bistro on Ocean Beach Road - twice-baked soufflé, ocean-trout gravlax, and a wine list leaning on small emerging French producers

Reserve a table +61 3 5984 4995
Bistro Elba · Sorrento

Bistro Elba is the Sorrento room that makes you feel like you have found the real version of the village. The space is deliberately low-key - timber floors worn soft, candlelight, a short menu chalked up every day - and the cooking is classic French bistro with enough restraint to make it feel entirely of its place. Twice-baked cheese soufflé, gin-cured ocean trout with cucumber and dill, whole flounder, a duck leg done slow and properly. Nothing on the plate is trying to be clever.

The wine list is the other reason to come. It leans into small, emerging French producers - natural-adjacent, cleanly made, and priced so you are not punished for ordering a second bottle - and the team knows every label well enough to hand you something you have never heard of and trust you will love.

Book for dinner on a Friday in autumn, sit at the bar if the dining room is full, and order the soufflé twice. It is that kind of place.
