Balnarring Bakehouse

The village bakehouse for the Balnarring side of the Peninsula — pies, pastries, and the breakfast sandwich that fuels the Saturday market crowd

Balnarring Bakehouse · Balnarring

Balnarring Bakehouse is the village bakery the Balnarring side of the Peninsula has relied on for years. The pies and pastries are proper country-bakery standard — a solid beef-and-mushroom, a honest custard tart, a vanilla slice that will not disappoint anyone over the age of eight — and the weekend breakfast sandwich has become the quiet fuel source for half the Balnarring Market crowd on a Saturday morning.

There is nothing aspirational about the room. That is exactly why it works. Go on a Saturday morning, grab a coffee and a breakfast sandwich, and walk across to the market with one hand on the paper bag.

A reliable anchor for any Balnarring-side weekend.
