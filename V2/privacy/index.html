Privacy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 10 April 2026

Peninsula Insider is an independent editorial publication. We don't run display ads, we don't sell data, and we don't use third-party trackers. This policy explains the small amount of data we do collect and what happens to it.

What we collect

Newsletter subscribers

When you sign up for The Insider's Dispatch, we collect your email address. That's it. We don't ask for your name, location, or anything else. Your email is stored with our email service provider and used solely to send you the newsletter.

Contact form and email

If you email us — story tips, corrections, editorial enquiries — we receive whatever you choose to send. We store those emails in our editorial inbox and use them only for the purpose you wrote to us about. We don't add you to any mailing list unless you separately subscribe.

Analytics

We use privacy-respecting, cookie-free analytics to understand which pages are read and how visitors find the site. This data is aggregated and anonymous — we cannot identify individual visitors. No personal data is collected through analytics.

What we don't collect

  • We don't use cookies for tracking or advertising.
  • We don't run retargeting pixels.
  • We don't collect payment information (we don't sell anything — yet).
  • We don't build user profiles.
  • We don't share, sell, or rent your email address to anyone, ever.

Third-party services

The site is hosted on GitHub Pages. Fonts are served by Google Fonts, which may log standard server request data (IP address, browser type) as described in Google's Fonts privacy FAQ. We have no access to that data.

Newsletter delivery is handled by our email service provider, which processes your email address under a data-processing agreement with us. Your email is not shared with other clients or used for any purpose beyond delivering our newsletter.

Your rights

You can unsubscribe from the newsletter at any time using the link at the bottom of every email. Once unsubscribed, your email address is removed from our active list.

If you'd like us to delete any data we hold about you — including past emails you've sent us — write to hello@peninsulainsider.com.au and we'll action it within 30 days.

Australian Privacy Act

Peninsula Insider complies with the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs) under the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth). We are a small publication and the data we collect is minimal, but we take the obligations seriously.

Changes to this policy

If we change this policy in any material way, we'll note the update date at the top of this page and, where practical, mention it in the newsletter. We won't retroactively weaken your protections without consent.

Contact

Questions about this policy: hello@peninsulainsider.com.au
