Paradigm Hill

George Mihaly's fastidiously farmed single-site Pinot Noir estate - appointment-only tastings with the winemaker

Paradigm Hill · Main Ridge

Paradigm Hill is a single-site Pinot Noir and Chardonnay estate run by George and Ruth Mihaly, and the tasting experience is deliberately small in scale: appointment-only, usually led by George himself, and structured around the kind of slow walk through a single year's wines that you only get at properties where the founders are still on the ground.

The wines are among the more cerebral on the Peninsula - a house style that leans into acid and structure rather than immediate fruit, and a single-vineyard approach that rewards cellaring. The Col's Block Pinot Noir is the bottle to take home and set aside.

This is a cellar door for people who want to spend an hour with the person who made the wine. Book well ahead.
