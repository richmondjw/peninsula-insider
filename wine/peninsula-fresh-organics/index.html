Peninsula Fresh Organics

A working certified-organic market garden with a farm-gate shop - straight-out-of-the-ground vegetables and the best salad of your week

Reserve a table
Peninsula Fresh Organics · Mornington

Peninsula Fresh Organics is the producer behind a lot of what ends up on Peninsula restaurant plates, and the farm-gate shop is open for anyone who wants to source their own ingredients straight from the ground. The range rotates with the season - leafy greens, roots, herbs, tomatoes in summer, brassicas in winter - and a small dairy fridge alongside the vegetables holds Peninsula-made cheese, butter and yoghurt when available.

The shop is utilitarian and the prices are honest. This is not a polished retail experience; it is the working end of a serious organic farm, and that is exactly its charm. Stop in on your way to a Peninsula kitchen and you'll cook a better dinner than most restaurants on the ridge.

Check the opening days before you drive - the farm operates to a working schedule rather than retail hours.
