Two Bays Brewing Co

Australia's first dedicated gluten-free brewery and taproom - surprisingly good pale ales and lagers, and a welcome destination for anyone the brewery tour has left out

Reserve a table +61 3 5981 4205
Two Bays Brewing Co · Dromana

Two Bays Brewing opened as Australia's first dedicated gluten-free brewery and has spent the years since quietly proving that the category does not have to taste like a compromise. The pale ale, lager, and session IPA are all genuinely good beers judged against any yardstick, and the taproom is the most welcoming destination on the Peninsula for anyone who has spent years sitting out the brewery tour.

The room is small and unfussy. Snacks are the brewery standards - pizzas, a burger, a charcuterie board - and the team pours with pride in what they are doing. For visitors with coeliac disease or a gluten sensitivity, this is the Peninsula's most inclusive beer stop; for everybody else, it is still worth the flight as an interesting comparison with the gluten-containing neighbours.

Check the website for current hours - Two Bays runs tighter than some of the bigger breweries.
