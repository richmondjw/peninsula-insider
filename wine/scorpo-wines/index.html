Scorpo Wines

Paul Scorpo's tightly focused family estate making some of the Peninsula's most acclaimed Pinot Gris alongside elegant Chardonnay and Pinot Noir

Book a tasting +61 3 5989 7697
Scorpo Wines · Merricks

Scorpo is a landscape architect's vineyard, and it shows. Paul Scorpo planted the estate in 1997 on a gently sloping block between Merricks and Dromana, designed the layout himself, and has spent the years since building one of the most tightly focused family wine operations on the Peninsula. The range is small - Pinot Gris, Chardonnay, Pinot Noir, Rosé - and each of those bottles is taken unusually seriously.

The Pinot Gris is widely regarded as one of the best in the country: textural, phenolic, aged on lees long enough to carry real depth. The Estate Pinot Noir and the Bestia Chardonnay are the two bottles other Peninsula winemakers mention when asked what they drink at home.

Cellar door visits are by appointment. This is not a drop-in cellar door; it is a proper tasting-room experience for people who already know what they like and want to go deeper.
