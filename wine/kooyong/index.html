Kooyong

Single-vineyard pinot noir and chardonnay from one of the Peninsula's most decorated estates

Kooyong · Main Ridge

Kooyong is the Peninsula's most consistently serious producer of single-vineyard wine, and the cellar door reflects it. The estate sits on the Main Ridge plateau with forty-odd hectares under vine, split into named single blocks - Haven, Meres, Ferrous, Faultline - that have given the Peninsula some of its most collectible bottles of the past fifteen years.

The tasting room is smaller and more contemplative than the trophy estates, and the flight through the range is a proper education: side-by-side pinot noirs from adjacent slopes show what micro-terroir actually looks like in Victoria's cool-climate heartland. The chardonnay program runs just as precise.

Book the single-vineyard tasting. Go midweek if possible. This is where you come to understand what the Peninsula tastes like at the top of its form.
