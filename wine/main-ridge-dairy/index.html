Main Ridge Dairy

A working goat dairy with a café - cheese boards, goat-milk gelato, and a view across the paddocks to the animals that made the lot

Main Ridge Dairy · Main Ridge

Main Ridge Dairy is a working farmhouse goat dairy that has built a small but devoted following for its farm-made cheeses and, more recently, its goat-milk gelato. The setup is simple: a modest café with a view across the paddocks to the herd, a cheese board built around whatever is ready on the day, and a scoop window running gelato flavours made from the farm's own milk.

It is one of those Peninsula stops where the appeal is directly tied to how small the operation is. You see the paddock. You see the animals. You eat the cheese. The kids understand the whole thing in about thirty seconds. Everybody leaves slightly more informed and slightly better fed.

A good fifteen-minute detour on a hinterland loop. Ideal with a chocolate stop at the nearby Sunny Ridge or Red Hill Cheese room.
