Advance Mussel Supply

Port Phillip blue mussels straight from the lease - cooked on site in pots the size of your head, or packed on ice to take home

Reserve a table +61 3 5979 1666
Advance Mussel Supply · Hastings

Advance Mussel Supply is the reason anybody who knows Peninsula seafood drives to Hastings. The mussels come straight off the company's own lease in Western Port, the quality is as good as blue mussels get in Australia, and the tiny utilitarian takeaway out the front will cook them for you on site - huge pots of garlic-white-wine mussels with bread on the side - or pack them on ice to take home.

The experience is deliberately unromantic. Plastic tables, a quick service line, and the point is the quality of what is on the plate rather than any attempt at atmosphere. For the home cook, the take-away pack is the move: two kilos of mussels, a bag of ice, and a Peninsula kitchen to cook them in.

Check opening days before you drive; the place runs to a working schedule.
