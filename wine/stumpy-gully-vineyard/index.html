Stumpy Gully Vineyard

A family-run estate with one of the Peninsula's broadest varietal ranges - Sangiovese, Marzemino and Picolit alongside classic cool-climate whites

Book a tasting +61 3 5978 8429
Stumpy Gully Vineyard · Moorooduc

Stumpy Gully is the antidote to a day spent tasting the Peninsula's usual Chardonnay-and-Pinot line-up. The Zuber family has been planting unusual varieties here for years - Sangiovese, Marzemino, Picolit, and a handful of others that barely exist anywhere else in Victoria - and the cellar-door flight leans directly into that experiment.

The estate is unpretentious, family-run, and genuinely welcoming. The tasting is relaxed and will give you a different view of what the Peninsula can do with Italian and central European varieties. Take it as your change-of-pace stop on a weekend otherwise dominated by the traditional producers.

Open most days - check ahead. Buy the Marzemino if it's available; it is unlike any other bottle you will take home from the Peninsula.
