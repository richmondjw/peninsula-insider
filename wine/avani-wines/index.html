Avani Wines

Shashi and Rohit Singh's biodynamic family estate - home to one of the Peninsula's only serious Syrah programs alongside Amrit single-vineyard Pinot

Book a tasting +61 418 555 941
Avani Wines · Red Hill

Avani is the Peninsula's quiet Syrah specialist, and that alone is reason to visit. Shashi and Rohit Singh run the estate as a genuinely small family operation - biodynamically farmed, hand-worked, and unusually focused on Syrah at a time when nearly every other cellar door on the ridge has all-in on Pinot Noir. The Amrit single-vineyard wines - Syrah, Chardonnay, Pinot Noir and a skin-contact white - are quietly among the most interesting bottles coming out of the region.

The cellar door is intimate and overlooks the estate's own vines. Shashi often pours, which turns a tasting into a conversation about biodynamics, Peninsula soils, and why a cooler-than-usual block can grow a Syrah that nobody saw coming. Avani was Gourmet Traveller's Best Small Cellar Door (Mornington) in 2023 and it earned every word of the citation.

Book ahead. Ask for the skin-contact. Bring home a bottle of the Syrah - it ages quietly and well.
