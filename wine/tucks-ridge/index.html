Tuck's Ridge

Founding Red Hill estate with one of the quieter great views on the ridge and a renewed focus on single-vineyard Pinot Noir

Book a tasting +61 3 5989 8660
Tuck's Ridge · Red Hill

Tuck's Ridge is one of the Peninsula's longest-established estates - planted in the mid-1980s, repositioned under a refreshed team over the past few years, and quietly making some of the most interesting single-vineyard Pinot Noir on the ridge. The cellar door has been gently modernised without losing the country-estate feel, and the deck view - down across the vines toward Western Port - is the kind of thing that stops new visitors mid-sip.

The Turramurra Pinot Noir is the bottle to pay attention to, the Chardonnay rewards the rebuild, and the tasting program now includes small-format food pairings on weekends that lift the whole visit beyond a straight cellar-door stop.

Check the website for current hours - Tuck's Ridge has been iterating on its opening pattern - and prioritise a weekend visit when the kitchen is running.
