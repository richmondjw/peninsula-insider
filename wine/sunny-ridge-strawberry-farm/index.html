Sunny Ridge Strawberry Farm

The Peninsula's original pick-your-own strawberry farm - seasonal berry picking, strawberry ice cream, and the best family stop in the hinterland

Reserve a table +61 3 5989 4500
Sunny Ridge Strawberry Farm · Main Ridge

Sunny Ridge is the Peninsula's original pick-your-own strawberry farm and has been the default family day-out on the ridge for decades. The season runs roughly November through April, and the routine is the same every year: pick your own strawberries in the field, weigh them in at the counter, and reward the work with a strawberry ice cream or sundae from the farm café.

There is no pretending this is a sophisticated food experience, and it is not trying to be one. It is a working strawberry farm that has figured out how to do hospitality without losing its soul, and the kids will remember it long after they have forgotten whatever cellar door you dragged them to afterwards. The café does a perfectly respectable coffee and a lunch menu for the grown-ups while the children chase berries.

Go in the morning on a sunny day. Wear clothes you do not mind staining.
