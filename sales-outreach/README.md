# Peninsula Insider — Sales Outreach System

Everything needed to run founding partner outreach for Peninsula Insider.

---

## Files

| File | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| `target-list.md` | Scored & tiered prospect list — every premium-fit venue on the Peninsula, ranked by brand quality, tourism reliance, marketing maturity, category fit, and budget. Tier 1 = target now; Tier 2 = target soon; Tier 3 = hold. |
| `outreach-sequences.md` | Full outreach sequences for 4 categories: fine dining, winery, boutique hotel, and spa/wellness. Includes cold email, follow-up, call opener, "send me something" script, and qualifying questions per category. |
| `partner-one-pager.md` | The document you send when a prospect says "send me something." Premium editorial tone. Covers what PI is, founding partner advantages, what partners receive, and the 3 partnership tiers with `[PRICE_1/2/3]` placeholders. |
| `crm-template.md` | Pipeline tracker with top 30 Tier 1 prospects pre-loaded, status key, and a per-contact detail block template for tracking every touchpoint. |
| `weekly-kpis.md` | Weekly tracking template — outreach activity, pipeline movement, conversion rates, revenue, and category breakdown. Copy each Monday, fill by Friday. |

---

## Outreach Workflow

```
1. RESEARCH    → Find contact name/email for target prospect
2. PERSONALISE → Write [PERSONALISATION] hook (2 min on their website)
3. EMAIL 1     → Send cold email from outreach-sequences.md
4. WAIT 5 DAYS → If no response...
5. EMAIL 2     → Send follow-up
6. CALL        → Use call opener script
7. QUALIFY     → Use qualifying questions to assess fit + tier
8. SEND        → If interested, send partner-one-pager.md
9. MEET        → 15-min call to walk through partnership
10. CLOSE      → Confirm tier, send agreement
```

Update `crm-template.md` after every step. Update `weekly-kpis.md` every Friday.

---

## Key Principles

- **Selective, not desperate.** Peninsula Insider chooses its partners — that positioning must come through in every interaction.
- **Editorial, not advertising.** We never say "ad placement" or "sponsored listing." This is editorial alignment.
- **Personalise everything.** Never send a generic email. Two minutes of research per prospect is non-negotiable.
- **Founding partner urgency is real.** The founding list is small and the advantages are genuine — communicate that without pressure.
- **Track everything.** If it's not in the CRM, it didn't happen.
