# Peninsula Insider — Founding Partner Overview

---

## The Mornington Peninsula deserves better than a directory.

Peninsula Insider is a premium editorial platform — a beautifully crafted guide for people who travel to the Mornington Peninsula with intent. People planning a weekend away, booking a special dinner, choosing between cellar doors, or discovering a spa they didn't know existed. We're the trusted source they consult before they arrive, and the guide they return to once they're hooked.

We are not a listing site, a deals aggregator, or a review platform. We are editorial-first: every recommendation is curated, every story is crafted, every partner is selected because they genuinely represent what makes the Peninsula exceptional.

---

## Why Now

Peninsula Insider is launching with a founding partner program — a deliberately small group of venues, wineries, stays, and experiences who will shape the platform from day one. Founding partners receive permanent advantages that won't be available once we open the program publicly: priority editorial placement, locked-in pricing, and input into how the platform evolves.

This isn't a media buy. It's a long-term editorial alignment with the platform that will define how people discover the Peninsula.

---

## What a Partner Receives

**Enhanced Listing**
Your presence on Peninsula Insider goes far beyond a directory entry. A rich, editorially written profile — your story, your offer, your positioning — presented in the visual language your brand deserves. High-quality imagery, key details, and direct links to your booking or contact channels.

**Priority Visibility**
Founding partners receive premium placement across the platform — in category pages, curated guides, seasonal roundups, and editorial features. When someone is looking for the best of the Peninsula, your name appears where it matters.

**Offer & Event Inclusion**
Running a winemaker's dinner? Launching a seasonal menu? Opening a new experience? Peninsula Insider features partner events and offers across the platform and in targeted content — reaching an audience that's already planning their visit.

**Editorial Alignment**
We don't just list you — we tell your story. Partners are woven into our editorial content: guides, itineraries, seasonal features, and discovery articles. This is earned-media-grade coverage, delivered consistently as part of your partnership.

**Reporting & Insights**
Regular reporting on your profile performance, audience engagement, and content reach. Understand who's discovering you, what's resonating, and where the opportunities are.
