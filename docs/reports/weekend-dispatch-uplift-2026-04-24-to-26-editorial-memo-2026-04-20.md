# Peninsula This Weekend uplift memo
## For: 24–26 April 2026 dispatch
## Date: 2026-04-20

### Recommendation
Reframe the dispatch around **Sorrento as the clear long-weekend base**, not just the dawn service as a standalone pick. The strongest shape is **ANZAC dawn + Writers Festival + Point Nepean**. That is more specific, more Peninsula-only, and more useful than a looser "dawn service + Cape Schanck + Hot Springs" mix.

## Best lead
### Sorrento as the ANZAC long-weekend base
**Keep the lead anchored in Sorrento, but sharpen the angle**: the real recommendation is not merely "go to the dawn service". It is **base yourself in Sorrento for Saturday morning's dawn service, then use the rest of the weekend for Writers Festival sessions and a Point Nepean half-day**.

**Why this is stronger**
- It turns three separate ideas into one coherent weekend shape.
- It is hyperlocal and hard to replicate elsewhere on the Peninsula this weekend.
- It matches official programming density in Sorrento across 23–26 April.
- It avoids asking readers to zig-zag across the Peninsula on a public-holiday weekend.

**Evidence / sourcing**
- Sorrento Portsea RSL is promoting a 2026 foreshore dawn service, gunfire breakfast, 11.30am procession, noon memorial service, and family BBQ.
- Sorrento Writers Festival official site confirms **23–26 April 2026**, with **155 events, 200 speakers, 10+ free events**, plus family and schools programming.
- Parks Victoria continues to position Point Nepean as the Peninsula's strongest history-and-landscape half-day, with shuttle, tunnels, Quarantine Station and Fort Nepean open to visitors.

**Editorial line**
The real play is: **wake early for the dawn service, keep Saturday soft, pick one festival session, and use Sunday morning for Point Nepean rather than another drive-heavy Peninsula errand.**

## Strongest cultural alternative
### Sorrento Writers Festival
**Keep it. Strengthen it.** This remains the best cultural inclusion by distance.

**Why**
- Officially locked for **23–26 April 2026**.
- Big enough to feel like a true anchor event, not filler.

Write it less as a generic festival listing and more as **the grown-up alternative for readers who want the long weekend to feel thoughtful rather than busy**.

Replace or demote **Cape Schanck** in this dispatch. It is a good Peninsula evergreen, but not the smartest fit for this particular weekend story.
