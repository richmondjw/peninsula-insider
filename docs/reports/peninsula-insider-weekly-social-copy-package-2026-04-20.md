## Publishing guidance

- **Facebook:** slightly longer copy is fine, especially when framing usefulness and weekend planning
- **Instagram:** keep the lead clean and quotable, pair with strong Peninsula imagery, carousel cover text should stay short
- **LinkedIn:** angle toward editorial strategy, local media differentiation, and product thinking rather than tourism language
- **Best sequence:** Tuesday newsletter push, Friday flagship traffic push, Wednesday and Sunday for brand authority
- **Visual system recommendation:** use one consistent weekly template for Peninsula This Weekend, one for category/vertical expansion, and one softer lifestyle template for Saturday/Sunday posts
