## Creative direction
Use a premium editorial style, calm and image-led.

### Day-by-day art direction
- **Day 1 / Journal:** cold-season atmosphere, road, coast, steam, fireplace, dusk water, or off-season village texture
- **Day 2 / Explore:** broad Peninsula planning composition, either collage or one iconic landscape with concise editorial headline
- **Day 3 / Eat:** produce-led rural dining image, table-side detail, vineyard lunch, kitchen garden, or estate dining room
- **Day 4 / Wine:** tasting-room or vineyard image with strong quiet authority, not a loud tourism shot
- **Day 5 / Stay:** room, property, or destination-base mood image that signals trip-shaping rather than generic accommodation promo

### Platform notes
- **Instagram:** strongest visual expression, integrated headline, save/share-led
- **Facebook:** practical readability, link-supportive
- **LinkedIn:** cleaner, more publication-like, less overlay treatment, caption does more of the work
