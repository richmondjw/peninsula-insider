# Peninsula Insider — Editorial System (Draft)

## House bylines
- **By James Richmond** — founder/editor voice, used for signed essays, editor letters, opinionated longform, high-trust flagship pieces
- **By The Peninsula Insider** — house byline for service journalism, guides, updates, and collaboratively produced platform content

## Recurring editorial formats
1. **The Insider Edit** — the strongest picks in a category, decisive, taste-led
2. **Weekend Notes** — weekly practical planning layer tied to current conditions and upcoming events
3. **Cellar Door Dispatch** — wine-country reporting, new releases, harvest notes, winemaker angles
4. **Long Lunch List** — restaurant-led curation built around mood, occasion, and booking urgency
5. **Stay Notes** — accommodation reviews and planning-oriented stay roundups
6. **Slow Peninsula** — walks, beaches, wellness, rainy-day, shoulder-season and reset pieces

## Voice rules
- Sound local, not tourist-board
- Be specific, not generic
- Use recommendations with judgment, not neutral summaries
- State who something is for, who it is not for, and what tradeoff comes with it
- Never invent human contributors; if the piece is not signed by James, use the house byline
