# Peninsula Insider — Free Image Sourcing Brief (Bridge Phase)

## Objective
Use free/licensed imagery only as a temporary bridge while preserving a premium editorial feel. Reject generic tourism wallpaper. Prefer clearly Peninsula-relevant, seasonally appropriate, compositionally strong images.

## Acceptance criteria
- Mornington Peninsula or visually plausible local geography
- Warm editorial colour grade or easy to grade into the current palette
- Strong focal point, not postcard clutter
- Human presence only when it adds scale or mood
- No cliché drone overload, no generic vineyard rows without place signal

## Priority image slots
1. Homepage hero
2. Eat page hero-support image
3. Wine page hero-support image
4. Stay page hero-support image
5. Journal / newsletter hero image pool

## Sources to mine
- Unsplash
- Pexels
- Wikimedia Commons (where rights are clean)
- Official venue media kits where reuse rights are explicit

## Rejection rules
- Obvious stock lifestyle clichés
- Locations that clearly are not the Peninsula
- Overprocessed sunset spam
- Images that look luxury-resort generic rather than region-specific
