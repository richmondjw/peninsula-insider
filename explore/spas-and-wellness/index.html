Day Spas and Wellness on the Mornington Peninsula

TL;DR

  • The Mornington Peninsula has the best spa-and-wellness offering of any region within 90 minutes of Melbourne.
  • Thermal springs (Peninsula Hot Springs, Alba) and treatment-focused day spas are different experiences — this page covers the treatment side.
  • Spa by Jackalope is the Peninsula's highest-design standalone spa. Book treatments well in advance.
  • Endota Spa Sorrento and Endota Spa Mornington are the accessible mid-range options with good treatment menus.

The Mornington Peninsula, Victoria, Australia has, within an hour's drive from Mornington to Cape Schanck, more genuine spa and wellness options than most Australian regions manage across a whole state. Thermal springs. Treatment-focused day spas in converted properties. Resort spa facilities integrated with accommodation.

This page covers the treatment-focused spa options. Thermal springs (bathing without treatments) are covered in the Peninsula hot springs guide.

Hot springs vs day spa: the distinction

A hot springs visit is primarily a bathing experience — thermal pools at various temperatures, outdoor soaking, no appointments. A spa day involves booked treatments: massages, facials, body wraps, hydrotherapy circuits with a therapist element. Many Peninsula operators offer both. This page focuses on those where the treatment experience is the point.

The spa options ranked

1. Spa by Jackalope — Merricks North

The Peninsula's most architecturally considered spa experience, integrated into the Jackalope hotel on the Red Hill–Merricks corridor. Treatment rooms are among the best-appointed in regional Victoria. Menu covers massage, facials, and body treatments. A non-guest spa day is bookable, though treatments sell out; book 2–3 weeks ahead on weekends.

Best for: Couples seeking a design-led spa experience. Those staying at Jackalope.

Price point: Premium.

2. Endota Spa Sorrento — Sorrento

The Sorrento outpost of the Endota chain — the Peninsula's strongest mid-market spa option. Good treatment menu, reliable quality, easy to book compared with Jackalope. Sorrento positioning means it pairs naturally with a morning back-beach walk or an afternoon at the Sorrento Ocean Baths.

Best for: Those based in Sorrento. Mid-range spa days. Uncomplicated booking.

3. Endota Spa Mornington — Mornington

Same operator as Sorrento. Slightly larger treatment menu. Good for those based in the northern Peninsula or combining with a Mornington town visit.

Best for: Northern Peninsula stays. Combining with a Mornington day.

4. Peninsula Hot Springs — spa treatments

Peninsula Hot Springs has an on-site spa treatment facility in addition to its bathing pools. The combination of thermal bathing and a treatment in a single visit is the most complete wellness day on the Peninsula. Book the spa element separately from the bathing entry.

Best for: Those already visiting Peninsula Hot Springs who want treatments added.

5. One Spa at RACV Cape Schanck — Cape Schanck

Resort-integrated spa at the RACV Cape Schanck property. Good for those staying at the resort or wanting to combine golf with a treatment. Standard resort spa quality.

Best for: Resort guests. Golf + spa combinations.

Building a spa day

The thermal-and-treatment day: Morning bathing session at Peninsula Hot Springs or Alba → afternoon treatment at Endota Spa Sorrento. This requires managing booking windows carefully.

The Jackalope day: Morning at Spa by Jackalope → lunch along the Red Hill corridor → afternoon winery session. The Peninsula's most elevated single-day combination.

The wellness weekend: The full built-out version is at the wellness weekend — a structured two-day itinerary with accommodation included.

FAQ

What is the best spa on the Mornington Peninsula?

Spa by Jackalope is the highest design standard. Endota Spa Sorrento offers the best mid-range experience. For a combination of thermal bathing and treatment, Peninsula Hot Springs' on-site spa is the most comprehensive.

Can you do a spa day on the Mornington Peninsula without staying overnight?

Yes. All day spas listed offer day-visitor bookings. Peninsula Hot Springs also accepts day visitors for bathing. Book treatments well in advance for weekends.

What is the difference between Peninsula Hot Springs and a day spa?

Peninsula Hot Springs is primarily a thermal bathing experience — outdoor pools at various temperatures. A day spa offers booked treatment sessions with a therapist. Full comparison at Peninsula hot springs guide.

Is Jackalope Spa open to non-guests?

Yes, treatments are bookable for non-guests. Availability is limited — book 2–3 weeks ahead for weekend slots.
