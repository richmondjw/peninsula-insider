Point Leo Estate Villas

A small collection of private villas inside the Point Leo Estate property - sculpture park access, Laura degustation on your doorstep, and the estate grounds all around

Point Leo Estate Villas · Merricks

Point Leo Estate's villas are the overnight continuation of one of the Peninsula's most complete destinations. The villas are small in number and private in layout - they share the estate's 16-acre sculpture park, the gardens, and the kitchen-to-lawn movement between the three restaurants, but each has its own enclosed terrace and a scale that makes them feel more like retreat accommodation than hotel rooms.

The stay makes sense because of what it unlocks: a Laura degustation booking without worrying about the drive home, a morning walk through the sculpture park before the day visitors arrive, and direct access to the Pt. Leo Terrace for a casual lunch without needing to leave the property. The wines are poured from the estate's own cellar at dinner; the Chardonnay program is as serious as anywhere on the Peninsula.

Book a villa around a Laura reservation. Walk the sculpture park at sunrise. This is the Peninsula's most considered one-estate overnight experience.
