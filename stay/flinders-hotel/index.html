Flinders Hotel

A polished village-base stay with proper pub energy downstairs and Bass Strait within easy reach

Check availability +61 3 5989 0201
Flinders Hotel · Flinders

Flinders Hotel works because it understands exactly what it is: a comfortable village anchor rather than a fantasy retreat. The rooms are contemporary, clean, and well-sized, and the location puts you within walking distance of Flinders' bakery, village shops, and the short drive down to the pier and coast.

The attached pub gives the whole property a welcome looseness. Stay here if you want to spend your days moving between the ocean side and the wine side of the Peninsula without ever feeling too far from either.

A smart base for couples doing the southern end properly.
