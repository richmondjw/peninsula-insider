Hillview Farm Cottages

Small, quiet cottages on a working Main Ridge farm - no hotel, no restaurant, just the plateau and a wood fire

Check availability
Hillview Farm Cottages · Main Ridge

Hillview is the counter-argument to design-hotel weekends. Three self-catered cottages on a working farm in Main Ridge, each one with its own paddock view, a wood fire, and enough kitchen to cook dinner rather than book one. The landscape does the heavy lifting: cool mornings, ridge fog, and vineyards visible from the deck.

The owners are hands-off in the best way. You check in, collect your eggs from the farm gate in the morning, and set the weekend's pace yourself. It is particularly good for a couple who want a break from restaurants, a small family wanting space, or anyone recovering from a week that asked too much of them.

Book the 'Ridge' cottage if it is available - it has the best view across the pinot blocks.
