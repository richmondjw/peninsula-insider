Alba Thermal Springs & Spa

The Peninsula's second-generation thermal springs complex - cleaner architecture, smaller crowds, better pacing than the original

Reserve a table +61 3 5911 2100
Alba Thermal Springs & Spa · Red Hill

Alba is the thermal springs the Peninsula had been waiting for. Where Peninsula Hot Springs has become a must-photograph theme park, Alba opened in 2022 with a deliberately stripped-back approach: thirty-one geothermal pools set into a quiet landscape in Fingal, a better spa building, and a ticketing system that caps bather numbers so the experience doesn't collapse into chaos.

The architecture is confident without being showy. The hot and cold circuits are properly sequenced. The on-site cafe is sensible. And the whole thing feels more like a European bath day than a tourist attraction - which is exactly what most Peninsula visitors want and rarely get from the category.

Book a morning session. Do the full thermal circuit properly rather than hopping. Lunch on the ridge afterwards and you have one of the most complete days the Peninsula offers.
