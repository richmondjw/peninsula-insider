Sorrento Coastal Retreat

A clifftop holiday house between the front and back beaches - the quietly perfect Sorrento weekend for couples who want space and a car nearby

Check availability
Sorrento Coastal Retreat · Sorrento

Sorrento is short on honestly-priced boutique accommodation that sits between the tourist hotels and the million-dollar holiday houses, and small retreat-style rentals like this one fill that gap. A self-contained clifftop house or pavilion, a five-minute drive to either the front beach or the back beach, enough kitchen to make breakfast, and enough privacy that you do not feel like you are sharing a building with anybody else. The category matters more than the specific name; look for this kind of listing on the Sorrento cliffs if you want a couples' weekend that runs on your own timetable.

This is the stay that works when you want the village on foot but not in your ear. The Sorrento ferry, the bakery, the gelato, and the back-beach cliff walk are all within a short drive or walk, and an evening booking at Bistro Elba or the Continental is a five-minute taxi.

Search the major holiday-house platforms for the clifftop-side listings. Book mid-week for the best rates.
