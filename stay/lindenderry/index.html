Lindenderry at Red Hill

40 acres of gardens, a small estate vineyard, and the kind of country-house feel the Peninsula doesn't offer anywhere else

Lindenderry at Red Hill · Red Hill

Lindenderry is the quiet, unflashy option on the plateau - and for many travellers it is the best one. A Lancemore property set on 40 acres of English-style gardens with its own small estate vineyard, its sensibility is country-house rather than design-hotel, and its weekend rhythm is set by the rose garden, the pool, and a genuinely excellent little restaurant that doesn't try to be anything other than what it is.

Rooms are comfortable and traditional, with a handful of suites that run to the garden. Breakfast on the terrace is one of the small pleasures of the Peninsula. The cellar door and tasting program are run with real care.

Choose Lindenderry when you want a weekend away that lets you decompress rather than perform.
