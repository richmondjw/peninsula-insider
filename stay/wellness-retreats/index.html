Wellness Retreats on the Mornington Peninsula

The Peninsula's wellness infrastructure is more serious than most people expect before they arrive. Peninsula Hot Springs is the largest geothermal bathing complex in the southern hemisphere. Alba Thermal Springs at Red Hill is a newer, design-led alternative with a more curated adult experience. Spa by Jackalope is a full resort spa operating at Jackalope Hotel on the wine ridge. RACV Cape Schanck's One Spa adds an ocean-view treatment experience on the south coast.

None of these feel like the hotel-spa-as-afterthought that fills most resort brochures. They're the primary reason many people come. The question is whether you want the thermal bathing experience centred (Peninsula Hot Springs, Alba), the spa-as-luxury-hotel experience (Jackalope, Cape Schanck), or something more restorative and slow (glamping adjacent to the springs, cottage with private garden).

Frequently asked questions

Can you stay at Peninsula Hot Springs?

The Springs itself is a day-visit facility — you cannot stay inside the complex. Peninsula Hot Springs Glamping operates adjacent to the property and gives overnight guests priority entry. It's the closest overnight option to the pools.

Peninsula Hot Springs vs Alba Thermal Springs?

Peninsula Hot Springs (Rye): larger, more varied, family-friendly, geothermal water. Alba Thermal Springs (Red Hill): smaller, design-conscious, adult-focused, cave pools and cold plunge. Alba suits couples better. Peninsula Hot Springs is better for a first visit or a group.

Where's the best full resort spa?

Spa by Jackalope (Red Hill) — six treatment rooms, thermal suite, and the whole Jackalope Hotel experience around it. One Spa at RACV Cape Schanck offers a full-service spa with ocean views on the south coast.

Is a car needed for a wellness weekend?

Almost always. Peninsula Hot Springs runs a weekend shuttle from Frankston station. Jackalope Hotel and Spa by Jackalope are co-located so you can walk between them — the only car-free wellness stay option on the Peninsula.
