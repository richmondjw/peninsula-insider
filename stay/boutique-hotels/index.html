Boutique Hotels on the Mornington Peninsula

The Peninsula's boutique hotels work best when they feel grounded in place rather than merely upscale. That usually means village access, architectural character, or a clear relationship to the hinterland and coast. Flinders Hotel, Hotel Sorrento, Lindenderry, and Jackalope all do that in different registers.
