Spa by Jackalope

The Peninsula's most design-led boutique spa — small, precise, and the natural pairing with a Jackalope stay or a Doot Doot Doot dinner

Reserve a table +61 3 5931 2500
Spa by Jackalope · Merricks

Spa by Jackalope is not trying to compete on scale. It is a tight, design-forward boutique spa inside the Jackalope Hotel vineyard, built to serve hotel guests and day visitors with treatments that match the broader Jackalope editorial: considered, unhurried, and quietly uncompromising.

The treatment rooms are dark, sculptural, and use Subtle Energies' Australian-made botanical range. The therapist roster is small and well-trained. Signature treatments include the Willow & Black Stone massage and bespoke facial work. There is no big wet-area circuit here — this is not a hot-springs-style complex. The value is in the depth of the one-on-one treatment, not in hours spent walking between pools.

The right pairing is obvious: book a spa treatment, stay the night at Jackalope, and have dinner at Doot Doot Doot. That is one of the most complete wellness-to-dining-to-stay weekends the Peninsula offers, and nothing else on the Peninsula delivers that exact combination at that level of design coherence.

For day visitors without a hotel booking: treatments are available, but the property is designed for guests first. The better single-visit experience for non-stayers is One Spa at RACV Cape Schanck or Alba Thermal Springs.
