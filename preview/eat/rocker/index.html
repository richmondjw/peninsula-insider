Rocker

One of Mornington's consistently busy brunch rooms — inventive eggs, a pastry cabinet worth the queue, and a small roastery's worth of specialty coffee

Rocker · Mornington

Rocker is one of those Mornington cafés that quietly became a weekend institution without ever quite trying. The menu does the modern brunch moves well — interesting eggs, a grain bowl that is not punishment, a signature breakfast sandwich — and the pastry cabinet is routinely better than anywhere else on the strip. The coffee program runs through a small roastery's worth of single-origin beans and the team on the machine has the pace of a city café.

Expect a short wait at 10am on Sunday. The queue moves, the seating turns, and the kitchen holds up. Go for the breakfast sandwich, order a pastry on the side, stay for a second coffee.

Use it as your Saturday-morning stop in Mornington before the day gets going. Good enough to be worth the queue.
