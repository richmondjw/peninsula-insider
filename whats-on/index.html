Editor's picks this month

If you only go to three events this month

The ones we'd happily stand behind in a group chat.

3 picks
10 April 2026 Wellness

Alba Thermal Springs - Fire & Ice Circuits

Red Hill

The quieter Peninsula thermal complex runs its hot-and-cold bathing circuit year-round, with dedicated silent sessions and sunrise slots that make the category make sense again.

Editor's verdict

The category has been broken by Peninsula Hot Springs' theme-park scale for a decade. Alba fixes it. Book the morning session, do the full circuit, eat on the ridge after.

Worth the drive First timer Adults only All weather
Read the full guide ->
12 April 2026 Live Music

Peninsula Hot Springs - Sunday Sessions

Rye

Live acoustic music every Sunday afternoon from the amphitheatre pool. Included with bathing. The category's most under-published recurring live music product.

Editor's verdict

The Peninsula's only weekly live music product that no other publication lists. Book a lazy Sunday afternoon, eat a late lunch on the ridge first, arrive at 2pm for the amphitheatre pool.

Worth the drive First timer Kids Grade B All weather
Read the full guide ->
18 April 2026 Racing & Sport

Mornington Cup Raceday 2026

Mornington

The Peninsula's biggest racing day of the season - eight races, a marquee lawn that runs like a long lunch, and a town that feels like it's on a day off.

If you come down for one event on the Peninsula this April, come for the Cup. It is the one day the town collectively takes the afternoon off, the food trucks actually mean it, and the 4pm light on the grass is the photograph you take home.

13 April – 15 April 2026 Nature & Walks

The Briars - Eco Explorers Autumn Sessions

Mount Martha

Council-run wildlife and bushland programming for families, tucked inside 230 hectares of wetlands, forest, and a working historic homestead. Free, underpublished, genuinely good.

Editor's verdict

One of the Peninsula's best-hidden free family outings. Run by council, not tourism - which is why nobody knows about it. Fix that.

Kids Grade A

25 April 2026 Civic

ANZAC Day Dawn Service - Sorrento Memorial

Sorrento

The Peninsula's most quietly moving civic event. A dawn service at the Sorrento memorial, a community breakfast at the RSL, and a village that drops its tourist costume for a morning.

Editor's verdict

If you're already staying in Sorrento for Writers Festival weekend, the Saturday morning dawn service is one of the most quietly moving hours on the Peninsula calendar. Not worth a special drive from Melbourne - worth going to if you're already there.

Kids Grade B All weather
