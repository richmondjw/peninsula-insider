## 🔴 HOT

### 1. Westernport Marine Environment Review — Submissions Close May 15
State government review of Westernport Bay marine environment is in public consultation. Outcome will affect shellfish, seagrass, and recreational fishing access. Small operators like the Kearney oyster operation are watching closely. **Covered this week** (oyster piece). Watch for interim findings.
- **Why it matters to readers:** Their bay. Their access. Their food.
- **Potential angle:** Broader round-up of what the review could change — written for people who eat fish, walk the shoreline, or sail.
- **Urgency:** Hot — submissions close in 3 weeks.

### 2. ANZAC Long Weekend — Peninsula at Peak Capacity
Accommodation fully booked across Sorrento, Portsea, Red Hill. Ferries running at capacity. Peninsula roads will be under pressure from Thursday afternoon. Visitor economy at full stretch.
- **Why it matters:** Our readers are locals navigating tourists. Practical intelligence is valuable.
- **Potential angle:** "How locals move this weekend" — quiet beaches, off-peak ferry times, where to eat without queuing.
- **Urgency:** Hot — this weekend.
